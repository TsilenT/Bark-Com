# Bonds & Relationships

## Overview
Units that survive together form **Bonds**, unlocking powerful passive bonuses when deployed together.

## Growth
*   **Mission Survival**: +1 Bond Score for every pair of units that survives a mission together.
*   **Combat Actions**: Certain actions (Heal, Avenge Kill) grants bonus bond points (+3 to +5).

## Bond Levels

| Level | Name | Score Required | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **Buddies** | **10** | **+5 Willpower** (Resistance to Panic/Psi) |
| **2** | **Packmates** | **25** | **+10 Aim** (When near partner) |
| **3** | **Soul Pups** | **50** | **Berserk Vengeance** (If partner dies, enter Berserk with bonus damage instead of Panic) |

## Nemesis System (Negative Bonds)
*   **Victim Log**: Enemies track who they have killed.
*   **Nemesis**: An enemy that kills a squad member may return in future missions as a "Nemesis" with upgraded stats and unique traits.
*   **Avenge**: Killing a Nemesis grants significant XP and morale bonuses.
