# Sanity & Panic System

## Overview
Sanity represents a unit's mental resilience against the horrors of the invasion.

*   **Max Sanity**: Typically **100**.
*   **Thresholds**: As Sanity drops, the "Fear Level" increases.

## Panic States
When Sanity drops critically low or specific triggers occur (seeing a terrifying enemy, witnessing a death), a unit may enter a **Panic State**.

### Types of Panic
1.  **Freeze**: The unit skips their turn (AP reduced to 0).
    *   *Duration*: 2 Turns.
2.  **Run**: The unit uncontrollably flees from enemies.
    *   *Duration*: 2 Turns.
3.  **Berserk**: The unit attacks the nearest target (Friend or Foe) with increased damage.
    *   *Duration*: 1 Turn.

### Recovery
*   **Natural Recovery**: Units have a **30% chance** to snap out of panic starting on the 3rd turn of panic (if it persists).
*   **Base Recovery**: Resting at the base recovers **10 Sanity** per mission skipped.
*   **Items**: **Dog Treats** or sedatives can restore Sanity in the field.

## Sanity Damage Sources
*   **Witnessing Death**: Seeing a squadmate die.
*   **Critical Hits**: Taking a critical hit from an enemy (-15 Sanity).
*   **Enemy Abilities**: Psionic attacks or terrifying roars.
