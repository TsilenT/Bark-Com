# Camera follows squad panel selection but not direct model selection

**Category:** UX
**Source:** TODO.txt #54 and #128 (consolidated)
**Original notes:**
- "User requested slight change to camera behavior \"ok so the squad panel selection should move the cam, direct model selection should not move cam\""
- "re-center camera on unit when clicked on in squad detail panel even if already chosen."

Selection rule:
- Click in squad panel → camera re-centers on the unit (even if already selected).
- Click the unit's 3D model in the world → camera does NOT move.
