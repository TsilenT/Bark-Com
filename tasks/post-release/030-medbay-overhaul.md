# Medbay overhaul (healing, forced stay, full-health-to-deploy)

**Category:** Feature
**Source:** TODO.txt #78 and the medbay block (lines 39, 42–43)
**Original notes:**
- "add healing to medbay"

- "Only full health units can go in to battle"

Bundle three related medbay mechanics:
1. Medbay actively heals units between missions.
2. Wounded units are forced to stay in the medbay for a random number of missions.
3. Only full-health units may be deployed on missions.
